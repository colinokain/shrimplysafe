The Application Process

Learn how to apply and configure your ShrimplySafe by following our easy step-by-step guide!.

Step Description
Step 1 Fill out and submit the application. This will include forms and supplemental items that imply nonrepudation.
Step 2 Once submitted, your application will be reviewed. Our team will continue the process upon acceptance.
Step 3 You will be assigned an API key in order to access the ShrimplySafe software.
Step 4 You can now start by storing new accounts, or transferring existing ones!

ShrimplySafe was designed to be a secure solution for account management on behalf of a first party. In order to ensure the security of our customers, the applicant must udnergo an application process. This process consists of basic information, as well as proof that is authentic of the applicant. Upon acceptance your company will be assigned an API key in order to access the ShrimplySafe software. Once this key has been assigned, you are now free to start storing safely, ShrimplySafe!

Security

ShrimplySafe, as the name implies, was built with the security of consumers in mind. As such we provide a solutions to companies who are seeking to outsource a storage solution for their accounts. These accounts are salted, hashed, and then stored. Once they are called upon login, they are validated by our API, and authenticated.
